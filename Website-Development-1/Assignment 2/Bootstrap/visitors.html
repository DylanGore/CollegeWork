Visitors

Please be aware that visitors are not allowed on the Little Saltee due to dangerous landing conditions!

Rules & Safety Concerns

  • Please pay careful attention to the cliff areas as the ground is unsteady and may give way.
  • No camping is allowed on the islands at anytime. The lighting of fires is strictly forbidden.
  • Drone aerial photography is strictly forbidden as it is intrusive and a danger to the birds.

Family in Residence

  • The family is in residence when the flag is flying. Please afford them privacy and do not approach the house.
